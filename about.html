The Game

Wooden Slide is a premium tactile puzzle experience designed for enthusiasts of classic sliding mechanics. Built with a custom physics-based engine, it replicates the weighted feel of physical mahogany blocks.

Premium Engine

Multi-Tile Drag 60 FPS Smoothness Solvable Shuffle Offline PWA High-Res Image Cropper

The engine supports advanced multi-tile sliding, allowing you to move entire rows or columns with a single fluid gesture.

Strictly Offline

This application is designed to work anywhere. No internet connection is required to play, save progress, or use the custom photo engine. Your data remains on your device.
