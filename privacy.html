Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Offline Operation

Wooden Slide is a strictly offline application. We do not use trackers, cookies, or any external analytics services.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, device identifiers, or usage data to any servers.

3. Local Storage

Your game progress, high scores, and dashboard statistics are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

4. Image Security

When you use the "Upload Image" feature, the image is processed locally in your browser. No images are uploaded to a server or stored permanently by the application beyond the current session's cache.

5. Third-Party Access

Since the app does not connect to the internet, no third parties have access to your activity or data within this game.
