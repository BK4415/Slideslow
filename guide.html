Game Guide

Objective

Rearrange the tiles by sliding them into the empty space until the numbers (or image) are in the correct sequence.

Multi-Tile Sliding

This is a premium engine. You don't have to move tiles one by one. If there is a gap at the end of a row, tapping the third tile in that row will move all three tiles at once!

Modes

  • Classic: Standard 1, 2, 3... order.
  • Snake: Numbers alternate direction each row.
  • Spiral: Numbers lead to the center.
